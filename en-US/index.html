Turn a site walk into a clear, professional report. SiteReportKit is an offline-first field inspection app for punch lists, snagging, defect tracking, handover, and closeout.

Record each issue with its location, trade, severity, assignee, due date, status, photos, captions, and non-destructive markup. Search and filter the list, update multiple items, and keep resolution notes and review history organized.

Export polished PDF reports, contractor action lists, closeout reports, and CSV issue registers. Choose report layouts, add company branding, and tailor action lists by area or assignee. No account is required for the core local workflow.

Go beyond basic markup with privacy blur and measurement annotations. Export editable XLSX workbooks as well as PDF and CSV, follow timestamped comments and activity, and exchange locked issue handoffs that merge returned updates. Draw, type, or import signatures. Original evidence is retained with SHA-256 integrity records.
