Billing And Purchases

  • SiteReportKit does not require an account or demo login for the current offline-first app.
  • The free app lets you create unlimited local reports and previews, with 3 watermarked PDF/CSV file exports included. SiteReportKit Pro is a one-time App Store purchase for unlimited clean exports, watermark removal, and custom branding.
  • Use Restore in the Pro dialog to restore a previous non-consumable purchase made with the same Apple ID.
  • App Store purchases, receipts, refunds, and charge issues are handled by Apple. If a purchase does not unlock after Apple confirms it, email support with your app version, device model, iOS version, and the approximate purchase time.
  • Project records, photos, signatures, PDFs, CSV files, and archives remain local unless you choose to export or share them.

Common Workflows

  • Create a project with client, site, inspector, branding, paper size, report type, and disclaimer details.
  • Add issues with area, trade, severity, assignee, due date, notes, and photo evidence.
  • Use photo markup and captions to clarify defects, punch items, snagging items, or closeout evidence.
  • Export full reports, contractor action lists, closeout reports, CSV registers, and project archives.
