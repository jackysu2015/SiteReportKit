The operational problem

SiteReportKit packages the same field data into a full client report or a lean outstanding-work list, so each recipient gets the appropriate level of detail.

Snagging often breaks down between capture and communication. Photos end up detached from notes, room names vary, and contractors receive a document that is difficult to filter. A structured snag record removes that ambiguity.

Who this is for: Residential inspectors, developers, client representatives, fit-out teams, property managers, and contractors completing handover or pre-completion inspections.

What a reliable field record needs

Every item should be understandable without relying on the inspector’s memory. Keep the description factual, make the location findable, show enough photographic context, and write the required action as an outcome that can be checked. Responsibility and due dates make the register actionable; status and closeout evidence make it traceable.

Field and report checklist

Adapt the checklist to your role, contract, jurisdiction, records policy, and the technical scope of the visit. SiteReportKit organises information; it does not confer inspection authority or make professional judgments for the user.

  • Room and location fields
  • Before and after photo evidence
  • Priority and status tracking
  • Trade and assignee grouping
  • Signature-ready report output
  • Local project archive

Report quality checks before issue

  1. Confirm the title, author, date, site, client, scope, and intended recipient.
  2. Check that item references are unique and locations use one consistent hierarchy.
  3. Remove duplicates and split conditions that require different owners or closeout decisions.
  4. Verify captions, annotations, names, dates, status, and required actions against the field record.
  5. Open the exported PDF, XLSX, or CSV, inspect image legibility, page breaks, filters, and counts, then store the issued version under the project’s records process.

Frequently asked questions

Questions about snagging app

Does the app work during a site visit with no signal?

Yes. The field workflow is offline-first, so you can capture locally and export when ready.

Can I give trades a shorter list?

Yes. Export a focused contractor action list rather than sending the entire client report.

Can reports use my project details?

Projects can include client, address, inspector, logo, accent colour, paper size, and report type details.

What is included in the sample report?

The downloadable interior sample includes a cover, summary, snag index, issue evidence, responsibilities, and closeout presentation.
